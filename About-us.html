Empowering Communities Through Service and Leadership

ENSA KHOURIBGA, KHOURIBGA, MOROCCO

Rotaract ENSA Khouribga is a student-led club affiliated with the National School of Applied Sciences (ENSA) in Khouribga, Morocco, and part of Rotary International's global network. The club organizes humanitarian initiatives, such as "Warm Winter" caravans and blood donation drives, focusing on community service and social impact. It also provides a platform for leadership development and international collaboration, sharing updates and engaging with the community on social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and Tiktok.
